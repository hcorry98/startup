About

Delligator allows teams to divide tasks to more easily manage a project. You can set up a project and then assign tasks—or parts of the project—to each member on the team. This lets you know exactly what portions of the team project each member is in charge of, and you can see in real-time everyone's progress with their responsibilities.

Delligator helps increase productivity and avoid confusion as to who is in charge of what. It also helps improve and ease communication about the progress of the project.

The mascot and logo for the app is an alligator named Del, Del the gator.
